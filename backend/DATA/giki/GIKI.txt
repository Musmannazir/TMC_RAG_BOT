# Ghulam Ishaq Khan Institute of Engineering Sciences and Technology (GIKI) — Overview
_Sample/dummy data for testing the multi-tenant RAG assistant. Not an official document._

GIKI is a private engineering and sciences university located in Topi, Swabi
District, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan. It is known for its residential campus,
strong engineering and computer science faculties, and industry-linked research.

## Academic programs (sample)
- BS Computer Science, BS Artificial Intelligence, BS Software Engineering
- BS Electrical, Mechanical, Chemical, and Materials Engineering
- MS/PhD programs across engineering and applied sciences departments

## Campus & student life (sample)
- Fully residential campus with student hostels
- Student societies (e.g., ACM, IEEE, AI Frontier Society) running technical
  events, workshops, and competitions
- Central library, engineering labs, and a innovation/entrepreneurship center
- Annual sports gala and technical festival

## Sample academic policies
- Attendance: minimum 80% attendance required per course to sit the final exam.
- CGPA requirement: students must maintain a minimum CGPA of 1.7 to avoid
  academic probation.
- Course withdrawal: permitted up to week 10 of a semester with advisor approval.
- Hostel check-in: students must sign in/out of hostel gates after 10:00 PM

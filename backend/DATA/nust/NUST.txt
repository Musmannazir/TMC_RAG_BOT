# National University of Sciences and Technology (NUST) — Overview
_Sample/dummy data for testing the multi-tenant RAG assistant. Not an official document._

NUST is a public research university headquartered in Islamabad, Pakistan, known
for its engineering, computer science, business, and applied sciences programs.
It comprises multiple constituent schools/colleges (e.g., SEECS, SMME, NBS,
NICE, S3H) operating under one central administration.

## Academic programs (sample)
- BS Computer Science, BS Artificial Intelligence, BS Software Engineering (SEECS)
- BS/BE Mechanical, Electrical, and Materials Engineering (SMME)
- BBA and MBA programs (NUST Business School)
- Postgraduate research programs across all schools

## Campus facilities (sample)
- Central library and school-specific labs
- On-campus hostels for men and women
- Sports complex, student societies, and an annual tech exhibition (NASCon)
- Career development office for internships and placements

## Sample student policies
- Attendance: minimum 80% attendance required to be eligible for final exams.
- CGPA probation: students falling below a 2.0 CGPA are placed on academic
  probation for one semester.
- Add/drop period: first 2 weeks of each semester.
- Hostel curfew: 10:00 PM on weekdays (sample policy for demo purposes).

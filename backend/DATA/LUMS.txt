# Lahore University of Management Sciences (LUMS) — Overview
_Sample/dummy data for testing the multi-tenant RAG assistant. Not an official document._

LUMS is a private research university in Lahore, Pakistan, widely known for its
business school (Suleman Dawood School of Business), law school (SAHSOL), school
of science and engineering (SBASSE), and school of education and social sciences
(SOE).

## Academic programs (sample)
- BSc Computer Science, BSc Economics, BSc Management Science
- BA-LLB (Law), BSc Electrical Engineering, BSc Chemical Engineering
- MBA and specialized master's programs across all schools

## Campus & student life (sample)
- Residential and day-scholar student population
- Extensive need-based financial aid program
- Student societies covering business, tech, arts, and social impact
- Library, innovation labs, and a dedicated career services office

## Sample academic policies
- Attendance: minimum 80% attendance required per course.
- Academic probation: triggered when cumulative GPA falls below 2.0.
- Add/drop period: first 2 weeks of a regular semester.
- Financial aid renewal: requires maintaining a minimum CGPA each year (sample
  policy for demo purposes).
